<Section icon={Swords} title="The Twining Combat System & Elemental Counters">

Combat in this RPG departs from traditional turn-based systems, opting for **real-time action**. The core of this system is **Twining**, which aligns the mind and body of the trainer with their companion creature.

### Combat Mechanics Table

| Mechanic | Action | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Attacks** | Hold Left-Click | Sustained elemental damage |
| **Active Skills** | Press Q / E | Cooldown-based special attacks or utility clones |
| **Ultimate Form** | Press R | Temporary evolution/digivolution with massive stat boosts |
| **Dodging** | Shift / Space | Invincibility frames to escape boss area-of-effect attacks |

### Elemental Type Chart
Understanding elemental relationships is vital for taking down high-level bosses and Alpha variants.

| Attacking Element | Defending Element | Damage Modifier | Tactical Advice |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Light** | Wind | Super Effective | Use against Wind bosses like Turbo |
| **Electric** | Wind | Super Effective | Great for breaking Wind shields |
| **Wind** | Dark / Grass | Super Effective | Excellent for clearing early-game camps |
| **Dark** | Fire | Super Effective | Counter aggressive fire-types |

<Callout type="success" title="Evolution Mechanics">
Unlike traditional games where evolution is permanent, some creatures utilize temporary combat-induced evolutions via their Ultimate skills, allowing them to return to their compact forms after battle.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression Walkthrough">

To quickly advance from a novice observer to an elite junior wayfarer, follow this structured progression route.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete the Polaris Enrollment">
    Complete the opening sequence in Starfall Valley, capture your first Ember Pup, and register your details at the Polaris Institute front desk to claim your official uniform.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Activate regional fast-travel Blooms">
    Locate and activate the fast-travel Blooms scattered across Breezy Plains. This clears the map fog and reveals nearby points of interest.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Level up your Starter to Level 12">
    Use growth buds and complete minor combat encounters to level up your starter. Evolve Ember Pup into Flame Ruff to unlock higher base stats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat Alpha Turbo for the Sky Pasture Badge">
    Equip a Light or Electric type companion. Challenge the Alpha variant, Turbo, in the Nimbus Fields. Lure it into heavy obstacles to ground it, then capture or defeat it to secure your badge.
  </Step>
</Steps>
