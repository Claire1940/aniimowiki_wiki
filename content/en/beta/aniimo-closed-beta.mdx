<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Solo Egg Heist Walkthrough">

Follow this systematic routing to maximize your loot efficiency while minimizing the risk of getting overwhelmed by elite patrols.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Coastal Warmup & Looting">
    Spawn on the beach and clear all basic chests. This builds your initial power, secures entry-level upgrade materials, and allows you to test your ability cooldowns.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Sanctum Infiltration">
    Enter the Sanctum corridors. Move slowly and watch out for wall needles and environmental traps, which can deal upwards of 2,800 damage per hit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Secret Chest Hunting">
    Search for cracked wall sections and metallic doors. Punch or strike these blockages to open secret rooms containing high-tier purple and orange chests.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Clearing the Egg Room">
    Locate the central egg chamber. Clear the guarding Aniimo patrols (usually 3-4 targets) before attempting to interact with the egg to prevent getting interrupted.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Extraction Sprint">
    Once you extract the egg, do not engage any further enemies. Follow the straightest path back to the boat and board immediately to secure your 70k+ gold haul.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Secret Openings">
