<Section icon={Swords} title="Role Classifications and Combat Functions">

Beyond elements, every creature in the database is assigned to one of four distinct combat roles. These roles define the creature's primary function in battle and determine their stat scaling and skill sets. A balanced team composition must incorporate multiple roles to ensure sustainability and damage output.

The four roles are DPS, Heal, BREAK, and REGEN. DPS units focus on dealing maximum damage, while Heal units keep the team alive. BREAK units are specialized in depleting enemy shields, and REGEN units accelerate energy recovery for the entire team.

| Role | Primary Focus | Key Stat | Recommended Position |
|-|-|-|-|
| DPS | Direct damage output | Attack | Backline |
| Heal | Health restoration | Max HP | Midline |
| BREAK | Shield depletion | Impact | Frontline |
| REGEN | Passive energy recovery | Speed | Midline |

<Callout type="tip" title="Synergy Bonus">
A balanced team should always feature at least one BREAK specialist to crack enemy shields before your DPS unleashes their main skills.
</Callout>

Understanding how these roles interact allows you to create powerful synergies. For example, pairing a high-speed REGEN unit with a heavy-hitting DPS unit allows for rapid skill deployment.

<Section icon={Target} title="Evolution Stages and Progression">

Creatures progress through three distinct evolution stages as they grow in power. Evolving your creatures is necessary to unlock their full potential, access advanced skills, and increase their base stats. The three stages are Lumin Stage, Gamma Stage, and Nova Stage.

Every creature starts at the Lumin Stage. Through training and resource investment, they can transition to the Gamma Stage, and eventually reach their final form at the Nova Stage.
