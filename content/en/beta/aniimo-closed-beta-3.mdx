Reaching the rank of Wayfarer is the ultimate milestone in **aniimo closed beta 3**. This promotion increases your level cap to 50 and unlocks high-tier capabilities. To qualify, you must complete your elite training, achieve a 40% area catch rate at Barine Causeway, and defeat Senior Pathfinder Lula in a midnight duel.

| Assessment Phase | Requirements | Rewards |
|---|---|---|
| **Elite Training** | Complete Chapter 3 checklist | Access to promotion exam |

Lula's team specializes in crowd control and high-area-of-effect (AoE) attacks. Using Helium is highly recommended due to its high defensive stats and powerful counter-attacks. Keep your distance when her Animo channels their signature breaking skills, then strike during their recovery frames.
