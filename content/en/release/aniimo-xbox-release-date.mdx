Aniimo blends creature collection with action-oriented combat and environmental puzzle-solving. Players use **Aniipods** to capture wild creatures, which can then be trained, evolved, and used in battles.

| Element | Primary Counter | Secondary Interaction | Common Habitats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
