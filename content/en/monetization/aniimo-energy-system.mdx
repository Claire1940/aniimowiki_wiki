<Section icon={Clock} title="Energy Recovery and Replenishment">

Managing your **aniimo energy system** requires a clear understanding of time-based regeneration. Prime Energy recovers passively over time, but players can also use active methods to refill their tanks when preparing for intensive farming sessions.

The passive recovery rate is fixed at **1 unit per 10 minutes**. This means players must plan their sessions around long-term regeneration if they wish to avoid spending premium-adjacent currencies.

| Time Duration | Energy Recovered | Potential Activities |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1 Hour** | 6 Units | Partial crafting costs |
| **5 Hours** | 30 Units | 1 Alpha Chest opening |
| **8.5 Hours** | 50 Units | 1 Omega Chest opening |
| **33.3 Hours** | 200 Units | Full recovery from zero |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Passive Regeneration">
    Always ensure your energy is below the 200 cap before logging off. If you stay at 200, you are essentially wasting potential progress.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prime Battery Usage">
    Use **Prime Batteries** found in chests or earned through missions to instantly replenish large chunks of energy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Glimmer Exchange">
    In the Polaris Institute or specific outposts, you can exchange **Glimmers** (earned from Elite Training and Alpha defeats) for energy refills.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="The Feedback Loop">
Be careful with the Glimmer-to-Energy loop. Since you need energy to open chests that contain Glimmers, running out of both can stall your progress significantly. Always keep a small reserve of Glimmers for emergency energy.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Pay-to-Progress vs. Pay-to-Win">

There is a significant distinction in how the **aniimo energy system** handles monetization compared to other modern titles. While the system acts as a "stamina" mechanic common in mobile games, its current implementation in the 2026 beta focuses on the speed of progression rather than raw power.

- **Non-Gated Content**: You can still battle, explore, and complete the story without spending energy.
- **Cosmetic Focus**: Currencies like Fame and Fashion Vouchers are isolated from combat stats.
- **In-Game Alternatives**: Prime Batteries can be earned through gameplay, meaning a free-to-play player can still participate in high-end farming by managing their time effectively.

| Feature | Monetized? | Impact on Gameplay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Aniimo Capture** | No | Core gameplay remains open to all |
| **Stat Boosts** | No | No direct purchase of power |
| **Energy Refills** | Likely | Increases farming frequency |
| **Cosmetics** | Yes | Visual customization only |
