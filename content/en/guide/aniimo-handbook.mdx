<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Strategy and Twine Mode Transformation">

Combat in Idyll is dynamic, offering two distinct styles: **Command Mode** and **Twine Mode**. This **aniimo handbook** emphasizes the synergy between these two modes to overcome Alpha battles and high-level Pathfinders.

In Command Mode, you act as a strategist, directing your Aniimo to use specific skills and managing cooldowns. In Twine Mode, you physically merge with your Aniimo, taking direct control of their movements and unleashing real-time combos. This is essential for dodging heavy boss attacks and performing precision strikes.

**Type Matchup Quick Reference:**
- **Fire** counters **Grass**

- **Lightning** counters **Water**

<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Power: Carried Items and Runes">

The final layer of the **aniimo handbook** power progression involves **Carried Items** and **Runes**. These items provide massive flat stat boosts and percentage-based increases that can elevate your combat power from the 1,700s to over 2,400.

Carried items, especially Gold-tier ones, can be enhanced up to level 15. As you level these items, you unlock Rune slots. Runes themselves come with various stats like True Damage, Critical Damage, and HP.

| Rune Type | Primary Stat | Secondary Effects |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Offensive** | Attack / Crit | True Damage, Damage % Up |
| **Defensive** | HP / Defense | Damage Reduction, Shield Strength |
| **Support** | Regen / Speed | Cooldown Reduction, Healing % |

**Rune Orb System:**
By equipping high-quality Runes, you accumulate "Orbs" (Blue, Red, or Green). Reaching specific orb thresholds (e.g., 20 Orbs) unlocks **Advanced Effects**, such as additional Attack +2% or specialized elemental damage boosts. Legendary Runes provide the most orbs and are required for Tier 2 and Tier 3 effects.

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Upgrading carried items to level 15 requires a significant amount of resources. Focus on your primary combat Aniimo first before spreading materials across your entire roster.
</Callout>
