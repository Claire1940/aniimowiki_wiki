<Section icon={Cloud} title="Rare Forms and Weather Variations">

One of the most complex aspects of completing the **aniimo dex** is tracking down environmental variations. Unlike standard RPG monsters, many Aniimo change their physical appearance and elemental affinities based on the current weather or the biome they inhabit. For example, **Scorchhowl** possesses a rare Thunderstorm form that only appears during active weather events.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check Local Weather">
    Monitor the real-time weather icons on your HUD. Thunderstorms and Rain are the most common triggers for rare forms.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Specific Biomes">
    Some species, like **Budclaw**, have four distinct forms: Basic, Mudflat, Bay, and Beach (where it resembles a coconut).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger the Encounter">
    Enter combat during the weather event. If the creature has a weather-dependent form, it will register as a new variant in your Dex.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Limited Opportunity">
Weather-based forms like the **Rain form Luminelle** are highly time-sensitive. You often have only one shot to capture them before the weather clears. Always keep high-tier capture cards ready.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Evolution Mechanics and Split Paths">

A prime example is **Skippy**, which can evolve into the ice-based **Glacy**, the prankster **Pranky**, or the foliage-covered **Leafy**.
