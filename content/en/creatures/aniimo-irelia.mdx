<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Recommended Companions and Synergy">

Companions are the backbone of any build in this ARPG. For the blade-dancing playstyle, you want companions that offer fast engagement, crowd control, and burst damage support. The Dark-element companions, specifically Pawney and Ignitis, form the core of this strategy, while Wind-element companions like Nimbi provide the necessary speed and utility.

Pawney, armed with a broken sword and armor, offers excellent defense and courage buffs, making it the perfect initiator. Ignitis allows you to strike from the shadows, delivering massive critical damage to unsuspecting targets. Meanwhile, Nimbi helps you float through tight spaces and gather groups of enemies together.

| Companion | Element | Role | Key Attribute |
| Pawney | Dark | Melee Defense | Courage Buff |
| Ignitis | Dark | Burst Assassin | Shadow Strike |
| Nimbi | Wind | Mobility Support | Cloud Float |
| Scorchhowl | Fire | Offense Buff | Burn Damage |

Combining these companions allows you to trigger powerful elemental resonance effects. Having two Dark-element companions in your active party increases your critical strike damage, which directly enhances the effectiveness of your rapid blade attacks.

<Callout type="tip" title="Resonance Tip">

</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Combat Rotation and Skill Combos">

Executing the combat rotation correctly is what separates average players from masters of the blade-dancing style. The rotation focuses on entering the fight quickly, applying debuffs with your companions, executing a flurry of high-speed strikes, and swapping out before the enemy can counterattack.

The initiation phase begins with Wind companion skills to position enemies, followed immediately by Dark companion swaps to maximize damage output.

| Phase | Action | Companion Active | Effect |
| Initiation | Dash & Slash | Nimbi | Speed Buff & CC |
| Core DPS | Blade Dance | Pawney | Shield & Strike |
| Execution | Shadow Strike | Ignitis | Critical Burst |

During the rotation, pay close attention to your character's animation frames. Many skills can be canceled early by inputting a dash command, allowing you to transition into the next attack without waiting for the full animation to play out.

<Callout type="info" title="Invincibility Frames">
The swap animation for your active companion grants a brief window of invincibility frames (i-frames). Use this mechanic to dodge incoming boss attacks without spending stamina.
</Callout>
