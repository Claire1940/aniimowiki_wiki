<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Starter Progression and Resonance">

To maximize the potential of your **aniimo starters**, you must engage with the Resonance system. This process functions like character ascension, boosting base stats and granting Capability Points (similar to Effort Values in classic monster tamers) to specialize stats.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Catch Species Duplicates">
    Catch multiple wild creatures of the same species to obtain species-specific crystals.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Astronite Shards">
    Spend your daily energy on boss chests to accumulate Astronite Shards for ascension.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Ascend through Resonance">
    Use crystals and shards in the Resonance menu to increase your star rating and unlock Capability Points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Distribute Capability Points">
    Allocate earned points into specific stats to customize your creature's combat role.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Optimization Strategy">
Do not hyper-invest in the very first creature you catch. Potentials, personalities, and traits are determined upon capture. Save your premium resources for high-potential or elite variants.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Homeland System and Element Buffs">

The Homeland system is not just a cosmetic housing simulator; it is a critical source of passive combat power. By completing requests for local residents, you earn materials to craft special RV decorations. These decorations provide permanent stat boosts to specific elements.

| Decoration Type | Crafting Material | Element Boosted | System Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Solar Panel RV** | Overworld Collectibles | Grass / Fire | Homeland Requests |
| **Water Filter RV** | River Minerals | Water / Ice | Homeland Requests |
| **Engine Core RV** | Rare Metal Ore | Electric / Steel | Boss Loot Drops |

<Callout type="info" title="Hoarding Tip">
Never sell overworld collectibles or random items found during exploration. These are frequently requested in Homeland missions and are vital for crafting high-tier decorations.
</Callout>

</Section>
