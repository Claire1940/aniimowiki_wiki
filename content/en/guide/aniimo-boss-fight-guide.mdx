The secret to winning an endurance-based boss fight in Aniimo is the rotation. You cannot stay on your main DPS indefinitely because their energy will deplete, and they will eventually succumb to chip damage. The following steps outline the most effective rotation for high-level Alpha bosses.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Field Deployment">
    Start the fight by switching to Gracewing and dropping both healing fields. This creates a safe zone where your DPS can mitigate the boss's auto-attack damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Energy Farming">
    Switch to Thorn Blade and use basic auto-attacks to farm energy power. Avoid using expensive skills during this phase; focus on staying within the healing field.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Break Phase">
    Once the boss's break meter is low, unleash your high-damage skills like Thorny Impact. You should aim to shave off at least 2% of the boss's total health during every break window.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Panic Recovery">
    If any party member drops below 20% health, immediately swap to Gracewing and trigger the Ultimate. This provides a 25% health buffer, allowing you to reset the rotation safely.
  </Step>
</Steps>
