<Section icon={Target} title="The Prime Energy System & Pay-to-Progress">

While the game avoids direct pay-to-win mechanics, the inclusion of Prime Energy introduces a "pay-to-progress" element. This system acts as a stamina cap that regulates how often you can claim high-value rewards daily.

### Prime Energy Costs and Recovery

| Activity | Energy Cost | Reward Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Omega Chest** | 50 | High-tier Glimmers, Rare crafting items |
| **Alpha Chest** | 30 | Standard Glimmers, Upgrade materials |
| **Antipod Production** | Varies | Capturing devices |
| **Battle Sim Rewards** | Varies | Combat experience, Training materials |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Passive Regeneration">
    Prime Energy naturally recovers at a rate of 1 point every 10 minutes, capping out at a maximum of 200 points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Consumable Replenishment">
    Players can instantly restore their energy reserve by consuming a Prime Battery, which may be featured in future monetization plans.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Resource Feedback Loop">
    Be mindful of your energy spend. If you run out of Prime Energy, you cannot open Alpha or Omega chests, halting your Glimmer acquisition loop.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Gating Warning">
Because Glimmers are required to purchase advanced items, running out of Prime Energy directly limits your ability to farm high-tier gear and cosmetics during long play sessions.
</Callout>
