- **Lore Integration**: NPC comments that provide hints, tips, and world-building flavor.

<Section icon={Settings} title="Multiplayer and Quality of Life Fixes">

Multiplayer interaction is a cornerstone of the **aniimo discord** community. A common frustration is the inability to play with friends due to level gaps. The proposed **Level Matching** system would automatically scale players to the zone or party leader's level, keeping content relevant for everyone. Additionally, the "Egg Heist" mode is undergoing scrutiny, with players demanding equal loot distribution to prevent the mode from becoming a selfish solo grind.

| Feature Area | Requested Change | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Co-op Play** | Automatic Level Sync | Play with friends regardless of progression |
| **Egg Heist** | Shared Loot Distribution | Encourages teamwork over hoarding |
| **Inventory** | Remove Antipod Caps | Less friction during long hunting sessions |
| **UI/UX** | Local Time Zone Displays | Easier to track timed weather events |

**Additional QoL Improvements:**
- **Remote Shopping**: Adding Beaniebot to the Homeland or RV for buying items on the go.
- **Dialogue Control**: Player-controlled text advancement to help with reading speed.
- **Shiny Notifications**: Louder and more visual cues when a sparkling Aniimo is nearby.
- **Customization**: Full color control for clothing and furniture, including wood tones and sparkling effects.
