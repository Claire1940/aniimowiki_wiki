<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Combat Mechanics: The Stun Window">

The "Break" mechanic is a core pillar of Aniimo combat. Dealing enough damage of a specific type (usually Impact damage from a Break-role unit) will fill a stagger meter. Once full, the enemy enters a "Stun" state for several seconds.

**Stun Window Benefits:**
- The enemy is completely incapacitated and cannot attack.
- All damage dealt to the enemy is significantly increased.
- Perfect time to swap to your DPS and unleash Ultimate abilities.

| Mechanic | Effect on Enemy | Best Counter Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stagger** | Meter fills based on Impact damage | Break |
| **Stun** | Enemy is immobilized for 3-5 seconds | DPS |
| **Elemental Burst** | Extra damage based on chart advantage | Any (with correct move) |

<Callout type="info" title="Pro Strategy">
Save your high-energy Ultimate moves for the Stun window. Using them while the enemy is active is often a waste of resources, as the damage multiplier during a Stun state is significantly higher.
</Callout>

</Section>
