Understanding **aniimo how to play** begins with the core "Twine" mechanic. While the game allows you to let your Aniimo fight autonomously, manual control through Twining is essential for overcoming difficult boss encounters and high-level challenges. Twining allows you to inhabit your Aniimo, giving you direct access to their movement, basic attacks, and special abilities.

**Video Highlights:**
- Direct control (Twining) is significantly more effective than AI-controlled combat.
- Movement and attack speeds vary greatly between different Aniimo species.
- Test an Aniimo's "vibe" through Twining before committing rare upgrade resources.
- Elemental advantages are critical for clearing the Hollow Battle Sim.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Progression: Resonance and Capability Points">

Once you have selected your favorite team, you must engage with the Resonance system. Resonance acts as a tiered ascension path that increases base stats and, more importantly, provides Capability Points (CP). CP allows you to specialize your Aniimo’s stats, similar to EV training in other monster-collection titles.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Species Crystals">
    Catch multiple Aniimo of the same species to obtain specific crystals. Higher rarity catches provide more resources.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Astronite Shards">
    Spend your daily energy to open boss chests in the overworld. These shards are the secondary requirement for Resonance stages.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Resonance Tiers">
    Each tier grants a permanent stat boost and a pool of Capability Points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Allocate Capability Points">
    Distribute points into Attack, Defense, Speed, or HP based on the Aniimo's role in your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Resonance Tier | Required Shards | Stat Bonus | CP Granted |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1** | 10 Green Shards | +5% Base Stats | 10 Points |
| **Tier 2** | 25 Green Shards | +10% Base Stats | 15 Points |
| **Tier 3** | 15 Blue Shards | +15% Base Stats | 20 Points |
| **Tier 4** | 40 Blue Shards | +20% Base Stats | 25 Points |

<Callout type="success" title="Optimization Tip">
Wait until you catch an Aniimo with a "Rare" or "Elite" trait before hyper-investing your Astronite Shards. Traits are fixed upon capture and can significantly alter the Aniimo's performance in the late game.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Investment Strategy: Potentials and Personalities">

A common mistake when learning **aniimo how to play** is dumping all resources into the first creature you catch. Every Aniimo has hidden Grade Potentials, Personalities, and Traits that are determined the moment they are captured.

**Key Investment Factors:**
- **Grade Potentials**: Determines the growth rate of specific stats (S-Tier to D-Tier).
- **Personalities**: Provides a percentage boost to one stat while slightly decreasing another.
- **Traits**: Unique passive abilities that can define an Aniimo's niche (e.g., "Flame Aura" for extra burn damage).

| Resource Type | Priority | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Leveling Gems** | High | Primary Elemental DPS units |
| **Astronite Shards** | Medium | Resonance for Elite-tier units |
| **Skill Manuals** | High | Unlocking 3rd and 4th skill slots |
| **Gold/Currency** | Low | Buying consumables and basic gear |

<Callout type="info" title="World Exploration">
As you complete world exploration and fill your Antilog, your chances of finding Aniimo with higher Grade Potentials increase. Patience in the early game will save you thousands of resources in the long run.
</Callout>

</Section>
