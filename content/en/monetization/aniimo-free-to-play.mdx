### Creature Capture Mechanics

Capturing wild creatures requires tactical preparation. Throwing an Aniipod blindly will result in wasted resources. Use the following techniques to maximize your capture success rate:

- **Stealth Approaches**: Crouch and approach sleeping or unaware creatures from behind to get an initial capture bonus.
- **Break Stagger**: During active combat, focus on dealing BREAK damage to deplete the target's stagger bar before attempting a capture.
- **Time and Weather**: Certain species only emerge during specific times of day or weather conditions. Fire-type spawns are suppressed during rain, while nocturnal species appear exclusively at night.
- **Pod Tiers**: Save your premium containment tools (such as Ultra or Hyper pods) for high-level Alpha encounters or rare variants.

<Callout type="tip" title="Resource Management Tip">
Do not spread your Experience Gems too thin. Focus on leveling a core team of 3 to 4 companions. If you decide to bench an early-game creature, releasing them refunds **80% of invested Experience Gems**, making roster adjustments highly forgiving.
</Callout>

Combat in Idyll is a dynamic mix of tactical commanding and real-time action. Pathfinders can switch between two primary operational modes seamlessly during encounters.

### Command Mode vs. Twine Mode

Understanding when to control your character and when to control your creature directly is the key to mastering difficult boss battles and timed simulations.

| Combat Mode | Primary Control | Best Used For | Key Advantage |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Command Mode** | Pathfinder (Human) | Support, positioning, item usage | Coordinated team-wide skill execution |
| **Twine Mode** | Captured Creature | Direct combat, dodging, combos | Precise movement and high-frequency attacks |

### The Elemental Type Matchup

With nine elements available in the official index—including Fire, Ice, Dark, Lightning, Grass, Water, Rock, Wind, and Holy—learning the elemental wheel is critical. 

- **Fire** counters Grass and Ice, but is highly vulnerable to Water.
- **Lightning** disrupts Water-type defenses but struggles against Rock-type structures.
- **Dark and Holy** exist in a high-stakes mutual counter relationship, dealing massive damage to one another.
