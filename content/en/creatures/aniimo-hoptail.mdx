<Section icon={Swords} title="The Twining System and Combat Mastery">

The "Twining" mechanic is the heart of *Aniimo's* combat. By syncing your spirit with your **aniimo hoptail**, you temporarily fuse into a powerful warrior capable of devastating attacks. This is not just a stat boost; it changes your entire moveset.

**Key Twining Mechanics:**
- **Spirit Space**: A mental realm where the bond is forged.
- **Sync Rate**: Higher bonding levels lead to longer Twining durations.
- **Elemental Pound**: A specialized melee attack that consumes "Energy Pips" to deal massive burst damage.
- **Hustle**: A mobility skill that allows the fused form to move on all fours, dodging incoming projectiles with ease.

| Twining Ability | Effect | Energy Cost | Best Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pound Strengthen** | Massive physical slam | 3 Pips | Breaking Boss Shields |
| **Elemental Pulse** | AoE elemental wave | 2 Pips | Clearing Mob Groups |
| **Spirit Dodge** | Invulnerability frames | 1 Pip | Avoiding Ultimate Attacks |
| **Final Sync** | Ultimate cinematic finisher | Full Meter | Ending Elite Battles |

<Callout type="info" title="Combat Strategy">
During the Twining phase, your health is linked to your Aniimo's. If the fused form takes too much damage, the bond will break, leaving both you and your creature vulnerable.
</Callout>
