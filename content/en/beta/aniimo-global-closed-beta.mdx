<Section icon={Map} title="Egg Heist Step-by-Step Walkthrough">

Navigating the Sanctum requires patience and a systematic approach. Follow these steps to ensure you reach the Egg Room and extract successfully without losing your progress in the **aniimo global closed beta**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Beach Grind">
    Start on the beach to warm up and gather basic loot. This area is low-risk and allows you to test your current damage output against easy targets. Open every small chest to find initial Blue-tier upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Sanctum Infiltration">
    Move toward the Sanctum entrance. Once inside, the environment becomes more hazardous. Listen for the sound of chains, which indicates a nearby patrol. Avoid high-threat enemies like Rookie or Bonnie unless you are fully geared.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locating the Egg Room">
    Look for specific door openings that can be punched or broken. The Egg Room is usually guarded by a small group of Anemo. Clear these enemies before attempting to interact with the egg to prevent getting interrupted.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Securing the Egg">
    Approach the central pedestal and hold the interaction key (default: F). Be prepared for a potential ambush. Once the egg is in your backpack, your movement might feel more restricted—this is the most dangerous part of the run.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Extraction Sprint">
    Follow the straightest shot back to the extraction site. Avoid the wall needles and Umbras that spawn during the retreat. Once you reach the boat, submit your haul immediately to lock in your rewards and experience points.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Threat Name | Attack Pattern | Counter Strategy | Danger Level |
|-------------|----------------|------------------|--------------|
| Rookie | Ground Slam | Keep distance, use ranged | Moderate |
| Bonnie | Chain Pull | Smash F to escape, dodge roll | High |
| Lacy | Rapid Needle | Shield up, wait for cooldown | Moderate |
| Wall Needles | Static Hazard | Timing based movement | High |
