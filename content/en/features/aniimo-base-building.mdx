<Section icon={Target} title="Automation Strategy">

To stop manually harvesting crops, you must assign specific creatures to your facilities. The key is matching the creature's elemental power to the facility's needs.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Assess Facility Needs">
    Check the facility's required traits (e.g., Water, Earth, or Wind) via the management screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Assign Correct Creatures">
    Drag and drop creatures with matching traits into the work slots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Build Storage">
    Place a storage unit nearby so creatures can haul harvested items automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Monitor Production">
    Use the manage mode to change crop types or check on creature stamina levels.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Automation Level | Requirements | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Basic | 1 Water, 1 Haul creature | Automated wheat cycle |
| Advanced | 1 Water, 1 Haul, 1 Chop | Full wood and crop cycle |
| Optimized | High level creatures | Increased yield and speed |

<Callout type="warning" title="Worker Management">
Avoid overloading your base with too many creatures of the same type if they don't have the specific trait required for the active facility, as this wastes food resources.
</Callout>
