<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Combat Skills and Lunar Power Trait">

The effectiveness of **Aniimo Lunara** stems from her unique resource management system. Unlike many other creatures that rely solely on cooldowns, Lunara utilizes a sophisticated Energy Point (EP) system tied to her passive trait. Understanding how to cycle between her skills and normal attacks is the key to maintaining high DPS during prolonged encounters.

**Lunar Power Trait Breakdown:**
Whenever Lunara casts a combat skill, she accumulates 4 stacks of "Power of the Moon." Each subsequent normal attack consumes one stack to recover 1 EP and 1% of her maximum HP. This creates a rhythmic combat loop: cast a high-impact spell, then use four normal attacks to replenish your resources and health.

| Skill Name | Type | EP Cost | Power | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Attack** | Basic | 0 | 6 | Standard magical light projectiles from a distance. |
| **Lunar Eclipse** | Magical | 20 | 67 | Gathers moon power to deliver a series of AoE attacks. |
| **Lumin Bolts** | Magical | 30 | 70+ | Fires high-intensity bolts that pierce through enemies. |
| **Ultimate Moon** | Ultimate | High | Massive | A beautiful moon appears, dealing heavy AoE damage. |

<Callout type="success" title="Sustainability Tip">
Because Lunara recovers 1% HP per stack consumption, she is one of the few DPS units that can sustain herself without heavy reliance on healing items, provided you manage your stacks correctly.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Elemental Matchups and Strategy">

To master **Aniimo Lunara**, you must understand the elemental rock-paper-scissors system. As a Light-type, she occupies a unique niche that makes her devastating against specific enemy types while being vulnerable to others. Positioning is your greatest weapon; since Lunara is a "glass cannon" Ranged DPS, you must use the environment to stay out of melee range.

**Elemental Effectiveness Table:**

| Interaction | Strong Against | Weak Against |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Attacking** | Dark, Wind | Light, Lightning |
| **Defending** | Light, Fire, Water, Grass | Dark |

**Strategic Positioning Tips:**
- **Kiting**: Always move backward or sideways between normal attacks to maintain the maximum distance from enemies.
- **Twining Mechanic**: When "Twined" with Lunara, your character gains her ranged capabilities. Use this to snipe priority targets from across the battlefield.
- **AoE Clustering**: Wait for enemies to group up before unleashing "Lunar Eclipse" to maximize the value of your EP expenditure.

<Callout type="info" title="Expert Observation">
While Lunara is weak against Lightning when attacking, her defensive profile is surprisingly robust, offering resistance to the four primary elements: Fire, Water, Grass, and Light. This makes her more durable against elemental mages than most other DPS units.
</Callout>

Leveling up **Aniimo Lunara** requires more than just experience points. To unlock her true potential, you must engage in Resonance Training at specific level milestones. This process requires Astranite, a resource found throughout the world of Aniimo.
