<Section icon={Swords} title="Creature Catching and Twining Mechanics">

The core gameplay loop of **aniimo on ps5** revolves around capturing creatures and utilizing their abilities through a system known as Twining. Unlike traditional creature-collecting games where your companions only fight alongside you, this game allows you to literally merge with them. By Twining with a captured creature, you assume their form, gaining their perspective, movement capabilities, and combat skills.

To capture these creatures, you must utilize specialized devices called Aniipods. Successful captures require more than just throwing a device; players must study creature behaviors, set tactical traps, and time their throws perfectly to maximize their chances.

| Creature Class | Twining Exploration Skill | Combat Advantage | Puzzle Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Aerial** | Sustained Gliding | Ranged Wind Attacks | Activating High Switches |
| **Aquatic** | Deep-sea Diving | Water-based Crowd Control | Extinguishing Fire Obstacles |
| **Terrestrial** | Underground Burrowing | Heavy Physical Defense | Breaking Cracked Walls |
| **Elemental** | Hazard Immunity | High Burst Damage | Powering Ancient Generators |

Twining is essential for overcoming environmental obstacles. For example, if you encounter a wide chasm, Twining with an Aerial creature allows you to glide effortlessly to the other side. If you need to navigate a submerged cavern, Twining with an Aquatic creature grants unlimited underwater breathing and rapid swimming speeds.
