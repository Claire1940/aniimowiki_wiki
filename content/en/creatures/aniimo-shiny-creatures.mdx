<Section icon={Map} title="Unlocking Inferlupa: The Multi-Day Quest">

The journey to obtain **aniimo shiny creatures** often starts with a base form. For Inferlupa, you must first secure an Emperor Pup and travel to **Beast Fang Ridge**. Unlike standard evolutions that occur via leveling, Inferlupa is locked behind a narrative quest that respects real-world time. You cannot rush this process with in-game items; you must wait for the server to "tick" over to the next day to progress the story.

The quest begins at a campfire in Beast Fang Ridge during the nighttime. You will encounter Ember Pups gossiping about a legendary alpha. Following this trail leads to a series of environmental interactions involving ancient chains and spooky campfire tales that expand over several IRL days.

<Section icon={Target} title="Material Farming: Flamer Ruff Spirits">

To evolve your **aniimo shiny creatures**, you need specific catalyst materials. For the Inferlupa evolution, the **Unbroken Flamer Ruff Spirit** is mandatory. A common mistake among new trainers is attempting to catch every Flame Ruff they see. However, the spirit item is a drop-only resource.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Special Spawns">
    Head to the specific sub-region of Beast Fang Ridge during the daytime. Standard Flame Ruffs found elsewhere will not drop the required spirit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Defeat, Don't Catch">
    You must reduce the Flame Ruff's HP to zero to trigger the loot drop. Catching them in a pod will result in zero spirit gains.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check Drop Rates">
    The Unbroken Flamer Ruff Spirit is a rare drop. On average, you may need to defeat 10-15 Flame Ruffs to secure the 6 spirits required for one evolution.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Navigate Day/Night Cycles">
    Remember that Flame Ruffs only appear in this specific area during the day. If it turns night, the spawns will swap to Bone Ski and other nocturnal creatures.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Optimization Tip">
Use a high-level "Break" character to quickly stun Flame Ruffs. This speeds up the farming process significantly, allowing you to clear more rotations before the day/night cycle shifts.
</Callout>

</Section>
